# Governance

crustyimg is an open-source project maintained by jysf. This document records the
commitments that make it safe to build on.

## Our promise: no rug-pull

**Every feature released under MIT OR Apache-2.0 stays that way.** crustyimg's
engine is free, permissive, and fully local — forever. Nothing you rely on today
will be taken behind a paywall or a login tomorrow.

If we ever offer a paid product, it will be one of:

- a **new or additive** feature built on top of the open engine,
- a **hosted or managed service**, or
- a **separate app for a different audience**

— **never** the removal of, or a gate on, what is already open. The permissively
licensed core is a durable commitment, not a trial. You can adopt it, fork it, and
build on it without worrying that the terms will change under you.
